Services

Claire has a wide range of professional design skills, with emphasis on versatility.

What Claire does can't be pigeon-holed into a single job title. Over the last 20+ years she’s worked in the capacities of Artist, Illustrator, Photographer, Print & Graphic Designer, Creative & Web Designer, Multimedia Designer, Digital Designer, UX & UI Designer, Visual Interaction Designer, Design Lead, Studio Manager, Creative Director...

Having such a broad range of industry experience and disciplines means that she fully understands the intricacies of any given project.

She likes to work collaboratively, and believes in good communication at all stages of a project, to ensure that you are completely satisfied with the end result.

Having provided design services for employers, as well as contracting/freelancing for clients, design studios and software houses, Claire has worked on many types of projects: From simple flyers to complex integrated UX projects; she's quick and versatile: She will impress you!



UI & UX

A well designed user interface will keep your audience engaged for longer on a website or app. It's not all about looks: To provide a slick experience (UX) it's important to understand the user.

Presentations

Shout about your company, product or service with interactive Flash and PowerPoint presentations. Impress your audience with slick charts, graphs, photos and illustrations in a polished presentation!

Print Design

The tactile qualities of printed media will never go out of fashion, from stand-alone corporate literature to fully integrated solutions. Flyers, datasheets, DM, brochures, packaging, posters, exhibitions...

Branding & Style

First impressions count: A strong brand identity is a simple way to gain immediate visual recognition. Bespoke brand guidelines will help you strengthen your brand throughout your corporate collateral.

Imagery

A picture tells a thousand words. Claire grew up using old-school pen and paper (paint brushes too), and has kept these skills alive, along with wacom illustrations and digital SLR photography.

Motion Graphics

Animation and video will get your message across with minimum audience effort and maximum impact. Synchronise visuals and commentary. Give a memorable visual twist to dull information.

Here’s how the process works

Discover

An informal chat to learn about you, your requirements and your audience; to establish your goals and decide which format suits you.

Define

After outlining strategy, deadlines and budgets we plan user journeys and build wireframes. Finalising these now minimises design costs.

Design

Our combined vision is translated into several creative concepts. You choose your favourite which is then polished until it sparkles.

Deliver

Working collaboratively with the client and developers, there are no nasty surprises. The result is a solution that everyone loves.
